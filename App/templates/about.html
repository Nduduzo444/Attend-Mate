About Attend Mate

Our Mission

Attend Mate is dedicated to creating a secure and efficient system for schools to monitor student attendance and communicate arrival and departure statuses with parents in real-time.

What We Do

Our platform provides a seamless way for school authorities to track students' attendance, ensuring safety and security, while parents receive timely notifications about their child's in-time and out-time.

Our Vision

To build a safer school environment by bridging the communication gap between schools and parents using technology.
